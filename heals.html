Terms: Boc = Box of chocolates
Bop = Bottle of pop
Med = Medkit
Band = Bandage

Note: Some items will not be on here as they have no priority (EVERYONE FOR THEMSELVES AHHH-)
Same goes for toons, if they are not on the list then they generally have less priority for these items (it rlly depends on your team)

Extractors (Vee/Rodger/Connie/Dazzle)

  • Boc
  • Speed Candy
  • Eject Button
  • Smoke Bomb
  • Pop/Bop (Sometimes)

Distractors (Pebble/Goob/Looey/Tisha/Flutters/Rudie/Coal/Razzle)

  • Pop/Bop
  • Protein Bar (Sometimes)
  • Airhorn
  • Bands
  • Meds

(IF NOBODY IS HURT OR AT RISK OF DYING DISTRACTORS GET HEALS)

Survivalists/All Rounders (Scraps/Toodles/Bobette/Teagan)

  • no priority haha

Supports (Sprout/Cosmo/Ginger)

  • Any Item (Sprout only for tapes)
  • Meds
  • Bands
  • Pop/Bop

IMPORTANT

Heals Such as Bands and Meds go to whoever needs them (1hp)
the priority for these items is only for when no one is at one heart (most of the time)

If no one is in danger distractors or supports should always take heals

Also mains are allowed to use Medkits (if another toon is also at one then it depends on the situation)
Example: If there is a Shelly and a Poppy both at 1 hp, Shelly should get the heal in this situation regardless of whether or not it is a medkit or band as she is more valuable to the team. however if there is a medkit AND a band, mains should get the band and save medkit for others
